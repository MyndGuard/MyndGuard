Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

🔒 The short version: MyndGuard processes all your browsing data locally on your device. We never sell, share, or store your personal browsing history on any server. Only anonymized, summarized wellness insights are ever sent to our AI provider.

1. Who We Are

MyndGuard is a Chrome extension developed by Vidya Suravarapu and Uma Suravarapu, students at Inventure Academy, Bengaluru, India. MyndGuard is designed to help families monitor digital wellness through browsing pattern analysis.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at myndguard@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Access

MyndGuard accesses the following data from your Chrome browser:

  • Browsing history — domain names and visit timestamps from the past 24 hours
  • Google account email — used only to send wellness email reports to the address you configure
  • Chrome storage — to save your settings and wellness scores locally on your device

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to generate wellness insights. Here is exactly what happens:

  • Your browsing history is collected and categorized locally on your device into wellness-relevant groups (e.g. entertainment, news, social media)
  • Only anonymized category summaries — never raw URLs or domain names — are sent to Groq's AI API to generate a wellness score
  • The wellness score and insights are stored locally on your device using Chrome storage
  • A wellness summary email is sent to your configured email address via EmailJS

📵 We never transmit raw URLs, domain names, or browsing history to any server. Only anonymized category counts (e.g. "15 entertainment visits, 3 news visits") are ever sent externally.

4. Data Sharing

We share anonymized, aggregated data with the following third-party services solely to provide the extension's functionality:

  • Groq API — receives anonymized category summaries to generate AI wellness insights. Groq's privacy policy is available at groq.com/privacy
  • EmailJS — used to send wellness email reports to your configured email address. EmailJS's privacy policy is available at emailjs.com/legal/privacy-policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

5. Data Protection Mechanisms

MyndGuard implements the following technical measures to protect your data:

  • Local-only processing — all browsing history analysis is performed entirely on your device using Chrome's built-in APIs. Raw data never leaves your device.
  • AES-GCM encryption — all API credentials stored within the extension (Groq API key, EmailJS credentials) are encrypted using AES-GCM with PBKDF2 key derivation (100,000 iterations, SHA-256). They are decrypted only at runtime, in memory, and never exposed in plain text.
  • HTTPS-only transmission — all external communications (to Groq API and EmailJS) are transmitted exclusively over HTTPS/TLS encrypted connections. No data is ever sent over unencrypted HTTP.
  • Anonymization before transmission — browsing data is anonymized into category summaries (e.g. "15 entertainment visits") before being sent to any external API. Raw URLs, domain names, and page titles are never transmitted.
  • No external database — MyndGuard does not operate any server or database. All wellness scores and settings are stored exclusively in Chrome's local storage on your device.
  • Minimal data collection — MyndGuard only accesses the minimum data necessary to generate wellness insights. No data is collected beyond what is required for the extension's core functionality.
  • Third-party security — Groq and EmailJS, our only two external service providers, both maintain industry-standard security practices. Links to their security policies are provided in Section 4.

🛡️ Summary: Your browsing data is processed locally, anonymized before any external transmission, and all API credentials are AES-GCM encrypted. MyndGuard is designed with a privacy-first, minimal-footprint architecture.

6. Data Storage & Retention

  • All browsing data is processed in memory and never written to any external database
  • Wellness scores and settings are stored in Chrome's local storage on your device only
  • You can clear all stored data at any time by removing the extension from Chrome

7. Children's Privacy

MyndGuard is designed to be used by families, including minors, under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The extension is intended to be installed and configured by a parent or guardian.

8. Permissions Explained

MyndGuard requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • history — to read your browsing history for wellness analysis
  • storage — to save your wellness scores and settings locally
  • alarms — to schedule automatic analysis every 8 hours
  • notifications — to alert you when a new wellness report is ready
  • identity / identity.email — to retrieve your Google account email for sending wellness reports

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Remove the extension at any time, which deletes all locally stored data
  • Opt out of email reports by not configuring an email address
  • Contact us at myndguard@gmail.com with any privacy concerns

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of MyndGuard after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
