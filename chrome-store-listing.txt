SHORT DESCRIPTION (132 characters max):

AI-powered mental wellness monitor for families. Understand your digital habits. Stay well. 100% private.


FULL DESCRIPTION:
-------------------------------------------------------
MyndGuard is a free Chrome extension that helps families understand the mental wellness impact of their digital habits — privately, compassionately, and automatically.

Built by two students from Inventure Academy, Bengaluru, MyndGuard monitors browsing patterns across 8 data sources and uses Groq's Llama 3.3-70B AI to generate a personalised wellness score every 8 hours.

🧠 WHAT IT DOES
MyndGuard analyses your digital activity across:
• YouTube watch history — detects emotional content themes
• Google Search queries — identifies stress or mental health signals
• Reddit activity — spots harmful community patterns
• Streaming services — flags binge-watching or escapism
• Shopping behaviour — detects impulse or stress-driven purchases
• AI tools usage — understands how AI is being used emotionally
• News consumption — flags doom-scrolling habits
• General browsing — categorises all other web activity

Every 8 hours, MyndGuard combines all signals and generates:
✅ A 1–10 wellness score
✅ Personalised insights and recommendations
✅ An automated email report sent to parents or guardians

🔒 100% PRIVATE
MyndGuard never stores or transmits your raw browsing history. All processing happens locally on your device. Only anonymized category summaries are ever sent to our AI provider — never URLs, never domain names, never personal data.

👨‍👩‍👧 DESIGNED FOR FAMILIES
MyndGuard was built with students in mind. It gives parents a gentle, non-intrusive window into their child's digital wellbeing — not surveillance, but care.

🌿 ALIGNED WITH UN SDG GOAL 3
MyndGuard is proudly aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3 — Good Health and Wellbeing.

📧 AUTOMATED WELLNESS REPORTS
Parents and guardians receive automated wellness summaries by email — no need to check the extension manually.
