What it monitors

Rich signals, compassionate insights

🌐

Browsing Patterns

Categorizes websites visited into wellness-relevant buckets. No raw URLs stored.

▶️

YouTube Watch History

Analyzes video titles and content themes to detect emotional signals.

🔍

Google Search Queries

Understands what topics are being searched — from learning to mental health concerns.

👾

Reddit Activity

Monitors subreddit visits to identify patterns linked to mood, stress, or mental health topics.

🎬

Streaming Services

Tracks time spent on Netflix, Spotify, Twitch, Disney+ and more to spot binge or escapism patterns.

🛍️

Shopping Behaviour

Detects impulse or stress-driven shopping patterns across Amazon, eBay, Etsy and more.

🤖

AI Tools Usage

Monitors visits to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and more — understanding how AI is being used emotionally.

📰

News Consumption

Tracks news sources and visit frequency to flag excessive doom-scrolling or anxiety-driven habits.

📧

Email Reports

Automated wellness summaries sent to parents and guardians every 6 hours.

🔒

Privacy First

Everything is processed locally. Only anonymized category data is ever sent to AI.

Installation

Get started in 3 minutes

MyndGuard is a free Chrome extension. Download the zip, load it in Chrome, and you're ready.

🧠 MyndGuard v1.0 · Chrome Extension

Download MyndGuard

Free for families, students, and educators. Works on Chrome and Chromium-based browsers.

⬇ Download MyndGuard.zip
🔒

Your browsing data never leaves your device. No accounts required. No data sold.

How to install

  1. 1

    Download the zip file

    Click the download button and save MyndGuard.zip to your computer.

  2. 2

    Unzip the file

    Extract the folder. You'll see a folder called mindguard-extension.

  3. 3

    Open Chrome Extensions

    In Chrome, go to chrome://extensions and turn on Developer Mode (top right toggle).

  4. 4

    Load the extension

    Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted mindguard-extension folder.

  5. 5

    Pin and open

    Click the puzzle icon in Chrome's toolbar, pin MyndGuard, and open it. Your Google account links automatically!

Our Story

Built by students, for everyone

Why we built this

MyndGuard was created because we noticed something: the adults around us cared about our wellbeing, but had no window into our digital lives — and we often didn't either.

We wanted to build something that bridges that gap. Not surveillance — understanding. Not control — connection. A tool that helps everyone have better conversations about mental wellness in the digital age.

We believe technology should serve our minds, not the other way around.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

MyndGuard didn't start as a Chrome extension. It started as a question — and grew one step at a time.

STAGE 1

The Question

It started with a simple curiosity — can technology understand how we feel? Vidya and Uma began exploring whether browsing patterns could reveal something meaningful about mental wellness.

💡
🎨
STAGE 2

Designing the Vision in Figma

Before writing a single line of code, the entire product was designed in Figma — mapping out screens, user flows, and the wellness dashboard. This became the blueprint for everything that followed.

STAGE 3

Building the Chrome Extension

Vidya architected the core extension using Chrome Manifest V3 — building the data collection engine that monitors browsing patterns across YouTube, Reddit, Google Search, and more.

🔧
🤖
STAGE 4

Integrating Groq AI

Uma researched and selected Groq's Llama 3.3-70B model as the AI engine — and configured the entire integration herself. The result: real-time wellness scoring that analyses 8 data sources every 6 hours.

STAGE 5

Adding Email Reports with EmailJS

Uma configured EmailJS to deliver automated wellness summaries directly to parents and guardians — giving families a gentle, non-intrusive window into digital wellbeing.

📧
🌟
